Justice for Everyone

The Global Justice Project is a community of individuals from around the world dedicated to ensuring free, fair and open access to justice. We believe that the protection of legal rights should not be dependant on a person’s resources or economic status.

Radical transparency

We’re open about what we do and how much it costs us to do it.  And we are confident of the fact that 100% of donations go directly to funding legal aid.  Don’t take our word for it – take a look for yourself!

Impact

We recognize the importance of local expertise.  So we partner with experienced international and local lawyers to identify casework that has high individual, social and developmental impact.

Community & innovation

We think that change and innovation always starts with people.  That’s why we want to build a community that engages and interacts with legal rights they care about.

Integrity

We believe that justice systems can be a tremendous force for positive change in society.  For that reason we promise not to compromise on the integrity of our mission – not even a little.  And we expect the same of all the lawyers and partners we work with.

- Andrew & Dom, February 9th 2016
